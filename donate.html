Prevention Is the
Highest Form of Public Safety.

Your gift turns prevention into practice — in schools, in courtrooms, and in the lives of people building a second chance.

Your donation helps James For Change Foundation bring prevention, education, legal support, and second-chance opportunities into communities that need them most.

Every school cannot afford to bring this work in. Every church cannot. Every jail, prison, youth program, or community organization does not always have the resources to provide meaningful programming, legal education, or reentry support.

James For Change Foundation exists because prevention is the highest form of public safety. We believe young people should see farther before the justice system becomes their teacher. We also believe people coming home deserve guidance, dignity, and real access to opportunity.

Every dollar helps. Whether your gift helps a student hear a message that changes their direction, supports a person trying to clear a legal barrier, or allows us to bring programming into a place that could not otherwise afford it, your donation becomes part of the work.
