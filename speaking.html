Speaking

He doesn't speak about
the system. He speaks
from inside it.

James White III brings a rare perspective to every stage: attorney, former prosecutor, justice advocate, and someone who has lived on both sides of the system. His message helps audiences understand why prevention, education, and second chances are not just inspiring ideas. They are public safety strategies.

Keynote Topics

What James speaks on.

Topic 01

Prisoner to Prosecutor

A powerful story of transformation, accountability, education, and what becomes possible when people are given the tools to change.

Topic 02

Education as Prevention

A message for schools and communities on why prevention must start long before a young person reaches a courtroom.

Topic 03

Reentry, Redemption, and Second Chances

A practical and personal look at what people need after incarceration, and why opportunity is one of the strongest public safety tools we have.

Topic 04

Public Safety Beyond Punishment

A challenge to rethink public safety through education, prevention, accountability, and long-term community investment.

Topic 05

Youth Mentorship and Decision-Making

A direct message to young people about choices, identity, discipline, and seeing beyond the moment they are standing in.

Why James

James White III is an attorney, former prosecutor, criminal justice advocate, founder of the James For Change Foundation, and Chair of the State Bar of Georgia's BASICS Committee. His writing has appeared in The Atlanta Journal-Constitution, and his work focuses on prevention, education, reentry, and second chances.
